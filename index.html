Three Square Coin Game

Player 1 (Black)'s turn
Player 1 (Black) 9 coins
Player 2 (White) 9 coins

Game Rules

  1. Each player has 9 coins.
  2. Players take turns placing their coins on the board's intersections.
  3. When a player forms a line of 3 adjacent coins (horizontal or vertical only, not diagonal), they can remove one of their opponent's coins. The opponent's coin cannot be part of a line.
  4. After all coins are placed, players take turns moving their coins to adjacent positions.
  5. When a player forms a new line by moving a coin, they can remove one of their opponent's coins.
  6. If all opponent coins are protected (part of lines), the turn is automatically skipped.
  7. A player wins when their opponent has fewer than 3 coins remaining or cannot make a valid move.
